Lord of the Mysteries

Lord of the Mysteries (诡秘之主) is a Chinese web novel set in a world blending Victorian-era steampunk with occult mysticism and Lovecraftian horror. The story follows Klein Moretti, a modern-day man who transmigrates into this fantastical world and quickly becomes entangled in the dangerous web of Beyonders — individuals who have consumed mystical potions to gain supernatural abilities, each bound to one of twenty-two Pathways leading from Sequence 9 all the way to Sequence 0, the rank of a True God.

Beneath the surface of a seemingly ordinary society lie secret societies, ancient gods, and cosmic horrors that defy comprehension. Klein navigates this treacherous world through cunning, wit, and an uncanny ability to improvise — eventually ascending to become a being that transcends the very system that governs the world.

The novel is celebrated for its intricate world-building, layered mysteries, and the gradual revelation of a cosmology that rewards attentive readers. It is widely regarded as one of the greatest works in the Chinese web novel genre.

About the Author
Cuttlefish That Loves Diving · 爱潜水的乌贼

Known by the pen name Cuttlefish That Loves Diving (爱潜水的乌贼), the author is a prolific Chinese web novelist who began serialising Lord of the Mysteries on Qidian in 2018. The novel concluded in 2020 with over 1,400 chapters and quickly became one of the highest-rated works on the platform.

The author's writing is distinguished by its meticulous attention to plot consistency, a vast cast of well-developed characters, and a genre-blending approach that draws from Western occultism, Tarot symbolism, Lovecraftian mythos, and steampunk aesthetics. The sequel, Circle of Inevitability (不可思议的诡秘之主), continues the story in the same universe.

About

Klein Moretti is the main protagonist of Lord of Mysteries, and an important side character of Circle of Inevitability. He is also the mysterious leader of the Tarot Club known as The Fool and a Transmigrator who comes from the modern era. He is the "Blessed" of the Evernight Goddess. He is the current owner of the Sefirah Castle, and the Lord of Mysteries with authorities over the Fool, Error and Door Pathways.From year 1352 of the Fifth Epoch, he became the God of the Church of the Fool.
